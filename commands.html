Quickshot Commands

Quick tip: qs is a shortcut for quickshot

Quickshot

Executing qs or quickshot will provide an overview of the available commands:

Quickshot #{VERSION}
==============================

Commands:
  quickshot configure                     Creates/Updates the configuration file in current directory
  quickshot pages                         Manage Shopify pages
  quickshot products                      Manage Shopify products
  quickshot theme                         Manage Shopify themes
  quickshot                               Show this screen.

Quickshot pages

Executing qs pages or quickshot pages will provide an overview of the available commands:

Quickshot pages #{VERSION}
==============================

Commands:
  quickshot pages upload [options] [filter]     Upload pages files, optionally providing a filter
  quickshot pages download [options] [filter]   Download pages files, optionally providing a filter
  quickshot pages watch [options]               Watch pages folder and synchronize changes automatically
  quickshot pages                               Show this screen.


Options:
  --target=[targetname]                         Explicitly select target for upload/download/watch
